B2B2C · vertical-ready SaaS

Give your teams and customers one place for commitments that actually ship.

Task-Tide is a hosted task workspace for businesses that coordinate work across internal staff and end customers — salons, clinics, field services, agencies, and other verticals where promises and follow-ups are the product.

Open the app API health

Built for honest positioning

We are not claiming to replace your CRM, calendar, or industry platform. Task-Tide focuses on a narrow slice: prioritized tasks, light AI nudges, and a clear picture of what is blocked or overdue — so you can validate product–market fit in one vertical before you scale distribution or pricing.

Hosted MVP

Sign up, create a workspace, and sync tasks from the browser. Authentication and multi-tenant storage are built in from day one so your pilot customers stay isolated as you iterate.

For the business (B2B)

Owners and leads see workload, priorities, and completion trends without exporting spreadsheets.

For the customer (B2C)

When you are ready, expose read-only or collaborative views so clients see status on their own work — without giving them your full admin stack.
